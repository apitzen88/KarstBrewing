Karst Maggie's Farm

Karst Maggie's Farm is the easy-drinking cream ale comfort food of the beer world. If you are new to craft beer, you will find security and comfort in Karst Maggie's Farm along with a very refreshing taste and smooth mouthfeel that will trigger an inner creativity through your tastebuds. Soon you may find yourself challenged to explore bigger beers! For you established craft beer lovers, Karst Maggie's Farm may very well be your "lawn-mower" beer go-to for quenching your thirst (and love of craft) on a hot summer day.

ABV: 5.4%

IBU: 16

Ember Waves

Situated on the eastern edge of the Great Plains we can appreciate the amber waves of barley, oats and wheat harvested and malted for this beer. With a touch of roasted barley and peated malt, there is a red hue and a subtle hint of smoke reminiscent of a glowing ember. Ember Waves is equally great around the camp fire or while kicking back after a hard-days work.

ABV: 7.6%

IBU: 22

Wry

Yes, the name is spelled correctly. With a mischievous addition of rye, you will taste a rich malt complexity with notes of licorice and raison to what otherwise might have been a hopped-up IPA. The 89 IBUs are much more balanced than the "perfect-in-style" IPA. By utilizing continuous additions of several hop varieties, Wry delivers a delicious spicy complexity to its balanced bitterness. Although we've identified Wry as a Rye IPA, it really defies classification, as the spelling may suggest. We love the tasty mischief that Wry offers and think you will as well.

ABV: 7.7%

IBU: 89
